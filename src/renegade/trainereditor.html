How to use it:


This is an editor allowing to generate the trainer list for my unified nuzlocke calc
  • Your work is automatically save upon switching position or adding a trainer to the list
  • The delete pokemon works instantly, if you made a mistake just switch trainer by using previous Trainer or Next Trainer
  • Boosts, HP aren't saved
